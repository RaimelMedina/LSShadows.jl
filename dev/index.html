LSShadows.UnitaryCircuit — Type

RandomCircuit object. The choice of single-qubit and multi-qubit gates that conform a layer is passed as an argument. Here we support Val(:XZXCnot)

Arguments

  • N::Int: Number of qubits
  • p::Int: Circuit depth

LSShadows.construct_unitary — Method
construct_unitary(f::UnitaryCircuit; val=Val(:XZXCnot))

Returns the unitary operator representing the N-qubit and depth p unitary circuit with the choice of layer given by the parameter type

Arguments

  • f::RandomCircuit: UnitaryCircuit object

Optional arguments

  • val: By default equal to Val(:XZXCnot). Other options are Val(:ZZX)

corresponding to the Floquet Ising Circuit and Val(:OnsiteHaar) for a local circuit composed of tensor products of Haar-random single qubit unitaries.
